Stop the madness

MyCryptoPass is a bridge that allows you to visit the distributed web of tomorrow in your mobile browser today.

Before, from your mobile to interact with decentralized apps you needed a dedicated web browser (e.g. Cipher) - You couldn't use your regular browser for all your browsing.

MyCrypoPass let you keep your browser taking the borring part out!
